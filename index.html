Q1. What industries can use Indane LPG?

We support a wide range of industries including hotels, restaurants, manufacturing units, metal processing plants, glassworks, and large-scale commercial kitchens.

Q2. What is Indane XtraTej?

Indane XtraTej is a specialized commercial LPG enriched with nano-additives. It offers a higher flame temperature, saving up to 10% on fuel costs and cutting down cooking time.

Q3. How do I book an Indane cylinder refill?

You can book a refill via WhatsApp (by messaging 'REFILL' to 7588888824), making a missed call to 8454955555, using the IndianOil ONE app, or via our web portal.

Q4. What are the safety guidelines for LPG leakage?

In case of a leak, turn off the cylinder valve, open all doors and windows, avoid switching on/off any electrical appliances, and immediately call our 24/7 emergency helpline at 1906.

Q5. Can I get a connection without address proof?

Yes! Our 5 kg "Chhotu" Free Trade LPG (FTL) cylinder is designed for students, bachelors, and migrant workers. It is available at select petrol pumps and distributors without address proof.

Q6. What is a Smart Composite Cylinder?

It is a lightweight, rust-free, translucent cylinder made of composite material, allowing you to easily view the remaining gas level and plan your next booking in advance.
